Recently they started the Brainbuster competitions, i.e. a series of interesting puzzles to be solved by mean of mathematical reasoning, computer programming, and so on. Brilliant!

What do you do after having entered one of their competitions ? Will you allow your solutions to be covered by dust on your hard disk ? No, of course; it's much better to let the web dust cover them ... after all, dust and cobwebs make a nice picture together.
