The (an)isotropic stocastic fractals

These are the stocastic processes that  I studied and developed in my Thesis.
Aren't they wonderful ? Sure they are! And so, when one wants to practice a bit of Java, there's nothing better than build an image generator that reveals them ...
How does it work ? Basically by constructing a realization of their spectrum and applying the inverse FFT (fast Fourier transform). I've simplified the algorithm since I didn't want to spend too much time translating from C++ to Java, but the result is nearly the same.

Here they are (the scrollbar on the right sets the Fractal Dimension: the more "ruvid" the image, the higher the fractal dimension).

Note: this applet may not work in your browser. JVM used by browsers are notoriously eccentric in their behaviour, and of course I can't pretend to have built an industrial-strength application...
